KOI DESSERT KITCHEN

For anyone as passionate about desserts as we are, the KOI Dessert Kitchen is the perfect place for you. Here, you can get the ultimate dessert experience – everything from sampling our signature creations and watching them get made, to learning to make them yourself

CAFE

Our main aim is still to provide you with delicious desserts to enjoy, which is why the space functions firstly as a café. Feel free to make yourself at home with your favourite sweet treat and a drink from the menu.

CLASSES

In 2017 KOI Dessert Kitchen was then created as the main production kitchen, expanding the KOI Dessert Bar brand. The kitchen also hosts Masterclasses within the venue showcasing KOI’s signature creations or hosts guest chefs where they share their skillsets.

MONKEY CORNER

Monkey's Corner is a small bar proudly brought to you by the Poernomo bros. Chill at the bar or make yourself comfortable in our dining room. Expect delicious food and refreshing cocktails. Discover an extensive collection of expertly curated boutique rum, whiskey, gin, and Japanese selections.
